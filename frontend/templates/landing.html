Platform Features

Everything you need for retinal care

Retinal Image Screening

Upload fundus photographs and receive an automated diabetic retinopathy severity assessment using our clinically-validated screening engine.

Diagnostic Focus Mapping

Visual overlay highlighting the specific retinal regions driving each severity assessment — giving clinicians clear, interpretable evidence.

Longitudinal Trend Tracking

Monitor DR progression across multiple visits with automated trend charts — catch deterioration early and adjust treatment plans accordingly.

Doctor–Patient Portal

Full two-sided portal — patients manage their own records while doctors get a complete clinical overview including high-risk alerts and inline note-taking.

Complete Scan History

Every screening is stored with the original image, severity result, confidence level, clinical notes, and timestamp — fully searchable and filterable.

High-Risk Alerts

Automatic escalation flags for Severe and Proliferative DR cases. Doctors are immediately notified of patients requiring urgent ophthalmologist referral.

Simple Process

How RetinaGuard works

1

Create Your Account

Register as a patient or doctor. Complete your medical profile in under 2 minutes.

2

Upload Retinal Image

Drag and drop a fundus photograph. Supports PNG, JPG, TIFF and BMP formats up to 16 MB.

3

Receive Screening Result

The screening engine analyses the image and returns a 5-level DR grade with a diagnostic focus map.

4

Track & Share

Results are saved automatically. Your assigned doctor can review and annotate every report.
