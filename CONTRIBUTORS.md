# Contributors

## Project Origin

RetinaGuard grew out of a diabetic retinopathy detection project built jointly by
Gaurav Singh Thakur and Niharika Raghunandan during their undergraduate studies.
The original system formed the academic foundation — the shared research, clinical
understanding, and initial detection model — that this platform extends.

RetinaGuard represents the next evolution of that work: a complete, production-grade
clinical screening platform with a full-stack web application, patient and doctor
portals, longitudinal tracking, and a diagnostic focus mapping system.
